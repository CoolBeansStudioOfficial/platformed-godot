# Introduction

Thanks for downloading the Platformed app! Here are the basic steps to get playing.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> If you are on Windows, you may get a screen like this when you open the app for the first time:
>
> <img width="200" height="200" alt="windows smartscreen" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/18b1be2e-af3c-4fa5-a853-f27372a61717" />
>
> If that happens, click `More info` and then click `Run anyway`.

When you open the app, you will immediately be presented with the Explore tab. The Explore tab shows levels uploaded by other users.

![explore](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/90335e57-13e1-44e5-a3a8-0ee0b612a763)

Clicking on a level will take you to its level page. Here, you can choose to play a level, download/remix it, or view its web version. 

![levelpage](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cef95700-666f-462f-85ab-a4cb35b61e53)

You will also be shown the user approval rating and completion percentage of the level. This can help you gauge the quality and difficulty of the level.

![ratings](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7ffd10ad-fb84-442e-85a7-04e7d7bfe64a)

# Controls

In Platformed, you control the player character.

![player](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3d193ce9-f9cf-4a52-8eb7-fdadac978409)

## Move

Move left and right with `A`/`D` or with the `←`/`→` arrow keys.

![move](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b7f9a5e8-792d-4511-b432-d72f519f13a6)

## Jump

Jump with `Space` or the `↑` arrow key.

![jump](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/11cf6560-4f2e-434e-8e1a-f0d3eade4671)

## Wall Jump

If the level you are playing has enabled it, you can press jump while touching a wall to jump off of it

![walljump](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b5cd1066-1c54-41ce-b663-b5af607d24f6)

# Settings

The settings menu is opened by pressing the cogs icon in the top-right corner of the app.

## Theme

The Platformed app implements a fully customizable theme system that allows you to create and share your own themes.

A theme consists of a background color and an accent color.

![themecreator](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/eea84aed-4511-419e-b796-27fd760a567e)
